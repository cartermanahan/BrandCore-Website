Right now, the full package at the launch price. That includes positioning, visual assets, content scripts, launch structure, promotion workflows, and exportable outputs in one purchase.

What is actually inside the brand pack?

Brand strategy, logo and visual assets, messaging, launch checklist, 30-day posting schedule, ad scripts, and a full ZIP export. The Inside the Pack section covers it in detail.

Do I need design or branding experience?

No. The app handles it. You put in the idea, the product does the rest.

Who is BrandFounder for?

Founders, freelancers, creators, and solo builders who want a brand pack without spreading the work across multiple tools and freelancers.

What happens if generation fails after purchase?

Your purchase stays linked to your account. Retry any time - no second charge.

Can I restore purchases?

Yes. Tap restore purchases in the app and your App Store state syncs back.

Who handles billing and refunds?

Apple. All purchases and refunds go through the App Store.

Do you store my brand data?

Yes - so your projects stay accessible across sessions and devices when you are signed in.

Do you collect analytics or review my inputs?

Only if it is enabled in your privacy settings. You can turn it off in the app at any time.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. The delete option is inside the app. The privacy policy covers exactly what gets removed.

Not yet. iOS is live now. macOS is planned.
