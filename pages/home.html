Everything inside your BrandCore pack

Think of it as the starter brand deck you wish you had: one place with enough clarity to move, not another “ideas doc” that lives in your notes app forever.

Brand strategy & positioning

Clear positioning statement, audience breakdown, value prop and brand story – written so you can explain it to a co-founder, investor or first customer without rambling.

Messaging & ad scripts

Landing-page structure, hero lines, bullets, email intros and multiple ad scripts tuned to your audience. Copy you can paste straight into your website and campaigns.

Visual direction & logo ideas

Colour palette, mood, typography direction and logo concepts. Enough for you or a designer to spin up a first visual system without staring at a blank Figma file.

Competitor & market snapshot

A concise view of where your idea sits versus similar products – what they’re saying, where they’re weak, and how you can position around them.

Launch & content plan

Suggested 2–4 week launch sequence, content themes and posting cadence so you’re not winging it day-to-day on social and email.

Export-ready PDF & text

A clean, downloadable PDF for your brand pack plus text you can copy into Notion, docs, your CMS or wherever you run your projects.
