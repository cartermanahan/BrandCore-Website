FAQ

Only the useful bits.

Is this actually generated inside the app?

Yes. The examples shown here come from BrandFounder’s real in-app generation flows. That includes brand direction, visual outputs, launch planning, and promotion structure. They are here to show the kind of work the product actually produces, not fictional agency case studies made outside the app.

What do I get for £12.99?

You get one complete brand package generated inside the app. That includes brand direction, positioning, messaging, pricing guidance, visual outputs, launch structure, and promotion workflows. In practical terms, it gives you something you can actually use to present the idea, publish content, and move toward launch, rather than leaving you with a few nice-looking graphics and no real plan.

What is actually inside the brand pack?

The pack is broader than just visual assets. It includes your brand foundation, market angle, pricing guidance, visual direction, ready-to-use branded assets, launch structure, and promotion support. Depending on the workflow, that can include logo output, social graphics, messaging direction, launch checklists, rollout plans, prompts, concepts, and creative guidance you can actually work from.

Do I need design or branding experience?

No. BrandFounder is built for founders, side-project builders, and early-stage operators who have the idea but not the time or specialist background to build a coherent brand pack from scratch. The app helps turn a rough concept into something clearer, sharper, and more presentable.

Who is BrandFounder for?

It is for people at the early stage of building something real: founders, solo builders, creators, consultants, and product people who want stronger branding and clearer launch direction without dragging the process out across multiple tools and freelancers.

What happens if generation fails after purchase?

If a purchase succeeds but generation is interrupted or fails, the paid package remains linked to your account so you can retry without paying again. You should not be charged twice for the same unlocked pack.

Can I restore purchases?

Yes. You can restore purchases through the app to re-sync your App Store purchase state on the device. This is useful if you reinstall the app, switch devices, or had an interrupted purchase flow.

Who handles billing and refunds?

Payments are handled through Apple’s App Store purchase system. Refunds are also handled by Apple under App Store policies rather than directly inside BrandFounder.

Do you store my brand data?

Yes. Saved brands, generated packs, and related assets are stored so they remain available across sessions and devices when you are signed in. That is what allows your projects to persist instead of disappearing after each session.

Do you collect analytics or review my inputs?

Analytics and optional input-sharing depend on your privacy settings in the app. If those settings are off, new create inputs are not added to the shared input collection. Privacy controls can be changed at any time.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. The app includes a delete-data option for removing local drafts, caches, and associated account data. The privacy policy explains this in more detail.

Is macOS available?

Not yet. iOS is the live focus right now. macOS is planned, but the current product and examples are centred on the iPhone experience.

Where do I find the app?

Once the listing is live, search for BrandFounder on the App Store or use the direct App Store link from this site. Until then, the site is here to show what the product does and what the in-app outputs look like.
