Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how BrandCore collects, uses and protects your information when you use the BrandCore apps (iOS and macOS) and the BrandCore website.

Controller: BrandCore (operated from the United Kingdom)
Last updated: 2 December 2025

1. Who we are

BrandCore (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides AI-assisted tools that help founders and small teams turn loose brand ideas into clearer positioning, messaging and first product directions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you use our apps and website.

For users in the United Kingdom and the European Economic Area (EEA), BrandCore is the “data controller” of your personal information for the purposes described in this Policy. We also rely on third-party providers (such as OpenAI, Firebase and email service providers) who act as “data processors” or independent controllers for some processing they perform.

2. Information we collect

We only collect the information we need to operate BrandCore, keep your account and projects working, and understand how the product is being used so we can improve it.

2.1 Information you provide to us

  • Account details – such as your email address and password (or sign-in method) when you create an account or sign in. Passwords are handled securely by our authentication provider and are not stored in plain text.
  • Brand inputs and project content – including brand names, descriptions, audience details, product ideas, notes and other text you enter when using BrandCore. This is the content we send to our AI providers to generate brand directions and messaging for you.
  • Early access and email – if you join the early access list or sign up for updates, we collect your email address and any preferences you provide (for example, product interest or platform preference).
  • Support and communication – if you contact us by email or through support forms, we may collect your name, email address and the content of your messages.
  • Optional feedback – if you take part in user interviews, surveys or feedback forms, we may collect your opinions about BrandCore and how you work. We try to keep this anonymised or aggregated wherever possible.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device and usage information – such as device model, operating system version, language, app version, basic event information (for example screens viewed and buttons tapped) and diagnostics. This helps us keep BrandCore stable, secure and easy to use.
  • Log and crash data – when the app or website encounters an error or crash, technical details may be logged (for example timestamp, error type and limited device information) so that we can identify and fix issues.
  • Website analytics – our website may use lightweight analytics to understand aggregated behaviour such as pages visited, referrers and basic device information. We do not use this to build individual marketing profiles.

2.3 Information from third parties

  • App stores – Apple (and, where applicable, other platforms) process your purchase and payment details when you subscribe. We receive limited information such as subscription status, region and anonymised performance statistics. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.
  • Authentication and storage providers – we may use services such as Firebase for authentication, cloud storage and analytics. These providers collect technical identifiers and usage information on our behalf to deliver the service.
  • AI providers – when you use BrandCore’s AI features, your inputs and related metadata are sent to our AI provider (such as OpenAI) so that they can generate outputs. These providers process data in line with their own terms and privacy documentation.
  • Email and support tools – if you subscribe to updates or contact support, our email and support providers process your email address and message content to deliver and track communications.

3. How we use your information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain BrandCore – including creating and managing your account, storing your brand projects, generating AI-powered outputs and syncing data between devices.
  • To operate AI features – by sending your inputs (such as brand descriptions, audience notes and product ideas) to our AI provider to generate responses, which we then display back to you in the app.
  • To improve the product – by analysing aggregated usage data and diagnostics so that we can understand how BrandCore is used, prioritise improvements, fix bugs and refine flows.
  • To communicate with you – for example about changes to BrandCore, updates to this Policy, product announcements, early access information and support responses. You can opt out of non-essential email updates at any time.
  • To manage purchases and subscriptions – including verifying your subscription status with app stores and unlocking any paid features tied to your account.
  • To keep BrandCore secure – for example by monitoring for abuse, preventing unauthorised access and enforcing our terms of use.
  • To comply with legal obligations – where we are required to retain or disclose certain information under applicable law.

4. Legal bases (UK & EEA users)

If you are in the United Kingdom or the EEA, we process your personal information under one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract – where processing is necessary to provide you with BrandCore and any paid features you have requested.
  • Legitimate interests – for example to maintain and improve BrandCore, protect against abuse, run basic analytics and communicate with you about product changes. We balance these interests against your rights and expectations.
  • Consent – where you actively give us permission (for example, if we introduce optional marketing emails or additional analytics controls). You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation – where we must process or retain information to comply with applicable law.

5. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share it when necessary to run BrandCore, when you ask us to, or when we are legally required to.

5.1 Service providers

We use trusted third-party providers to help us operate BrandCore, including:

  • AI providers – such as OpenAI, to process your brand inputs and generate outputs.
  • Cloud and database providers – such as Firebase or similar services, to store your account data, brand projects and usage logs securely.
  • Analytics providers – to help us understand how BrandCore is used in aggregate and to improve performance and stability.
  • Email and support tools – to send product updates, early access information and respond to support queries.
  • App stores – such as Apple, for app distribution, in-app purchases and billing.

Where these providers process personal data on our behalf, they are only permitted to use it to provide their services to us and are bound by contractual obligations of confidentiality and data protection.

5.2 Legal and safety reasons

We may disclose information about you if we reasonably believe it is necessary to:

  • comply with a law, regulation, court order or other legal request;
  • protect the rights, property or safety of BrandCore, our users or others;
  • detect, prevent or address fraud, security or technical issues.

6. International transfers

BrandCore is operated from the United Kingdom, but some of our service providers (including AI, cloud and analytics providers) may process data in other countries, such as the United States or within the EEA. Where personal data is transferred outside the UK or EEA, we aim to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses or equivalent protections, as required by applicable data-protection laws.

7. Data retention

We keep your personal information only for as long as we have a valid reason to do so – for example while your account or projects are active, while you remain on an early-access list, or as needed to meet our legal obligations.

  • Account and brand project data – kept while your account is active. If you ask us to delete your account, we will remove or anonymise your personal data within a reasonable period, subject to any legal requirements to retain certain records.
  • Early access and email list data – kept while you remain subscribed. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in our emails or by contacting us directly.
  • Analytics and logs – kept for shorter periods where possible and used primarily in aggregate form to improve stability and product quality.

8. Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit, secure authentication and strict access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is completely risk-free, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights over your personal information:

  • Access – to ask for a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction – to ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion – to request that we delete your personal data, in certain circumstances.
  • Restriction – to request that we limit how we use your data in certain cases.
  • Objection – to object to certain types of processing (for example, where we rely on legitimate interests).
  • Data portability – to request a copy of certain data in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent – where we rely on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@brandcore.app. We may need to verify your identity before responding. If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority. In the UK, this is the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).

10. Children’s privacy

BrandCore is designed for founders and adult professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without appropriate parental consent, in line with applicable data-protection laws. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information without consent, please contact us so that we can delete it.

11. Cookies and similar technologies

The BrandCore apps do not use traditional browser cookies. Our website may use a small number of essential cookies or similar technologies to keep the site secure and functional. Some of our providers (such as analytics or AI infrastructure providers) may use technical identifiers to deliver their services and measure usage in aggregate.

12. Third-party links

BrandCore may contain links to third-party websites or services, such as the Apple App Store, AI providers or external resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties, so we encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing them with your information.

13. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time to reflect changes in BrandCore, in the services we use or in applicable laws. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, provide additional notice in the app or by email.

Your continued use of BrandCore after any update to this Policy will constitute your acceptance of the revised terms.

14. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, you can contact us:

  • By email: support@brandcore.app
